Welcome to the Online Judiciary System

Access judicial services and case information online. The Indian judiciary is hindered by inefficiencies and delays due to manual case management, physical court proceedings, and limited adoption of technology. These issues lead to extended case backlogs, limited access to legal services, especially in rural areas, and a lack of transparency, which undermines public trust. Addressing these challenges requires a strategic shift toward digital transformation and technology-driven solutions to enhance efficiency, accessibility, and accountability in the judicial system.
